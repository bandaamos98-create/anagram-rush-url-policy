1. Introduction

Welcome to Anagram Rush, an engaging Android word puzzle game developed by Amos Banda.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App.

Effective Date: June 14, 2026

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and username if you create an account.
  • Game Data: Your game progress, scores, and preferences.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and unique identifiers.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the game.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage statistics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the gameplay experience.
  • To save and sync your game progress.
  • To manage your account.
  • To fix bugs and improve performance.
  • To respond to your inquiries.

4. Third-Party Services

Supabase – Authentication and cloud storage.

Google Play Services – Core Android functionality.

We may introduce advertising networks in the future.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

Data is only shared with necessary service providers (e.g., Supabase) under strict confidentiality.

6. Account & Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their account and all associated data at any time.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your email address and Supabase authentication account
  • User ID and any linked identifiers
  • All game progress, scores, and saved data

All data associated with your account will be permanently deleted.

How to Request Deletion

To request account deletion, please send an email from the registered email address associated with your account to:

bandaamos98@gmail.com

Subject: Delete My Anagram Rush Account

Secure Account Verification

You do not need to provide your password. Identity is verified using the email address linked to the account. We may send a confirmation email to verify ownership before proceeding.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are processed within 7–14 days.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in Supabase. Upon deletion, both authentication records and database records associated with your account are permanently removed.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

Anagram Rush is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
