1. Introduction

Welcome to Anagram Rush, an engaging Android word puzzle game developed with care for your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you download, install, and play Anagram Rush ("the App").

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about our data practices.

Effective Date: June 14, 2026

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you choose to create an account using Supabase authentication, we may collect your email address and username.
  • Game Data: Your game progress, scores, levels completed, and preferences when you choose to save them.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and screen resolution.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the game (play time, levels attempted, features used).
  • Local Storage: Game progress saved directly on your device.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage statistics to help us understand and improve the game.

We do not collect precise geolocation data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the gameplay experience.
  • To save and sync your game progress across devices (when logged in).
  • To authenticate and manage your account.
  • To analyze usage patterns and fix bugs or performance issues.
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Third-Party Services

Supabase

We use Supabase for user authentication and cloud data storage. Supabase processes your data under its own Privacy Policy.

Google Play Services

The App may utilize Google Play services for basic functionality such as push notifications or achievements. Google’s privacy practices apply.

Future Advertising

We may introduce advertisements in future updates. These may be served by third-party advertising networks that use your Advertising ID and other device data. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics tools to better understand how players enjoy the game.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share information only in the following limited situations:

  • With service providers (like Supabase) who help us operate the App and are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • For legal reasons (court orders, legal processes, or to protect rights and safety).
  • In the event of a business transfer (merger, acquisition, etc.).

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children’s Privacy

Anagram Rush is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information about a child under 13, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy within the App and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
